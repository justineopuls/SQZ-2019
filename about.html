THe National EEE COMPETITION AND CONVENTION

ABOUT SQUEEEZE

SquEEEze has been the gathering of the country’s top students and professionals in the field of Electrical and Electronics Engineering for more than two decades. With years of catering the students with challenges that will develop their skills in the said field, SquEEEze has continued to grow into what it is now.

Revamped with an EEE Convention and a Technological Design Contest, this 4-day event will not only test their minds in the field of EEE but also equip them with skills to adapt to the ever-changing landscape of the country.

NEW THIS YEAR:
Technological Design Contest

This year’s installment of SquEEEze includes a Technological Design Contest wherein students from across the country will be presenting their innovative ideas that provide solutions to the problems of our environment, community and country.

INTER-HIGH SCHOOL QUIZ SHOW

Amplify your potential! As early as high school, students must be aware of the various applications of math and science in the field of EEE. This year we’ll bring together the sharpest high school students from Region III, Region IV‐A and NCR, introduce them to the fundamentals of EEE, and put their knowledge to the test in the Inter‐High School Quiz Show.

INTER-COLLEGIATE EEE QUIZ SHOW

Test your abilities! The 22nd National Inter‐Collegiate EEE Quiz Show will pit the country’s cream of the crop EEE student teams against each other. Three rounds of tough questions and grueling problems await! In this battle of guts, intelligence, and strategy, who will come out on top?

CIRCUIT SPRINT

Boost your skill! Race your way to victory in Circuit Sprint. This competition will test your team’s practical skill, efficiency and intuition. With limited instruments, components, and time, teams of three, will need to build a circuit to match the given schematics and specification.

COMPANY TALKS

Company Talks will be a platform for our participants to interact with various potential employers and gain insights that will guide them in searching for internships and future jobs.

EEE Convention

SquEEEze 2018 National Convention will be a gathering of the country’s top minds in the field of Electrical and Electronics Engineering. There will be a breakout session in the afternoon where the largest tech companies from the Philippines gather in one place to provide insights of the current technological advancements. Speakers for the lectures will be composed of the biggest names in the tech industry. Attendees may have the opportunity to submit their resumes to the companies invited.
